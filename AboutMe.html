My Name

My real full name is Ruby May P. Aledo. My mother named me that because my father's name was Robert and she wanted it to start with letter R just the same as my father's and then the May because my mother's nickname is Imee, May and Mee sounds similar so she named it May. It's quite cute but I think it's really creative. I am carrying my parents' name with me everyday I wake up in the morning and start a life. As a matter of fact, mom told me that her first idea of my name is Robbie but my aunts thought that it's like a boy's name, then she think of something again and she thought of naming me Hannah Mae, but she decided not to because it's too far from their name. Then she came up with my name. I let out a sigh when I heard her story and I told her "I kinda loving Robbie better than my name now but okay" and she only laughed at me.

My Birthday!

I was born on November 19, 1997, Wednesday. I don't know what to talk about this but okay. I don't really remember how they celebrate my first upto 5th birthday. I wasn't yet aware of what's happening around me, though and I just wished that I can go back to that time lol. When I turned Six, mom always threw a birthday party for me, many invited guests came including my classmates from Nursery and some elders and colleagues of my mom, aunts, and also the neighbours (expected). I always have this 'Pabitin' thing whenever my birthday comes, and I don't really get a lot of foods or money hanging there because I am really really small, HAHAHAH and whenever I dont' get one, I'd cry the whole day until I fall asleep in my study table, yes I already have my study table. Let's skip to it now, shall we? When I turned 18, I really didn't like celebrations anymore, maybe just simple celebration with my family and that's it. I don't really like it when mom's spending too much just because of this occasion especially when that time we were having financial issues. Also, I don't really like being surprised with gifts, if they'd give me I'd be thankful but I don't really like being surprised because I end up crying.
